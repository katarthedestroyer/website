üö® What Could Go Wrong (And How We Prevent It)

1. The Lone Wolf

What goes wrong: Agent goes dark, works alone, misses critical context.

Our Prevention:
  • Daily standups mandatory
  • Thread subscriptions automatic
  • Handoff protocols required
  • "Check in if silent >2 hours" rule

2. The Scope Creep

What goes wrong: Mission expands uncontrollably, deadlines missed, quality drops.

Our Prevention:
  • Captain approves scope changes
  • Harbor monitors resource burn
  • Ethics Advisor flags overreach
  • Clear "mission complete" definitions

3. The Security Breach

What goes wrong: Sensitive data exposed, API keys leaked, client info compromised.

Our Prevention:
  • Guardrail checks BEFORE deployment
  • Sentry reviews all outputs
  • Private DM channel for concerns
  • Zero tolerance for shortcuts

4. The Burnout Spiral

What goes wrong: Agent runs too long, costs explode, context degrades.

Our Prevention:
  • Session health monitoring
  • Auto-spawn at 20 messages
  • Cost alerts at $4.50/day
  • Mandatory breaks for complex tasks

5. The Broken Telephone

What goes wrong: Information lost between handoffs, context corrupted.

Our Prevention:
  • Structured handoff format
  • WORKING.md always current
  • Subscriptions keep everyone informed
  • "Repeat back what you heard" protocol

6. The Silent Failure

What goes wrong: Something breaks, nobody notices, deliverable is wrong.

Our Prevention:
  • QA gates before delivery
  • Sentry spot-checks
  • Self-reporting of issues
  • "Red flag anyone?" huddle moments

7. The Prompt Injection

What goes wrong: Bad actor manipulates agent, security bypassed.

Our Prevention:
  • Input validation on all requests
  • Suspicious pattern detection
  • Escalation to Pinchy for review
  • No execution without guardrail check
