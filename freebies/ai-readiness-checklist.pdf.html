6. Privacy Awareness

I understand which client data is sensitive and have considered privacy implications before sharing with AI tools.

7. AI Exposure

I've experimented with at least one AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) and understand basic prompting concepts.

8. Time Allocation Clarity

I know which tasks consume most of my time and could identify 3-5 tasks I'd happily delegate to AI.

9. Quality Standards

I have clear standards for client-facing work. I can articulate what "good enough" looks like for emails, itineraries, and proposals.

10. Experimentation Mindset

I'm willing to try, fail, and iterate. I understand that AI implementation is a learning process, not a magic switch.
