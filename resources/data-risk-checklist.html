Email is the #1 source of data breaches. These checks ensure your communications are secure.

No credit card data sent via email Critical
Includes full numbers, partial numbers, CVV codes, and expiration dates
No passport numbers or SSNs in email Critical
Even "last 4 digits" can be problematic if combined with other data
No unencrypted PDF attachments with sensitive data High
Itineraries, confirmations, and forms should be password-protected
BCC used appropriately for group communications Medium
Client emails should never be visible to other clients
Email retention policy in place Medium
Old emails with sensitive data should be deleted per policy
Two-factor authentication enabled on email High
Gmail, Outlook, and all business email accounts
